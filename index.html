Imperial College Raising and Giving

About

RAG (Raising and Giving) is the fundraising arm of Imperial College Union, and we'd love for you to get more involved! Whether you've been doing this for years and have your own charity or want a hand with your on-campus fundraiser, or even if you're new to charity work all together we'd love to hear from you. The things that we do over the year include bungee jumps on Queens' Lawn, climbing Mt. Kilimanjaro, masquerade balls, cake sales, bike auctions and raids - but we're always up for trying something new so please do get in touch!
